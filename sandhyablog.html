Quiet Places

I don't usually write much anymore, but some places stay with you.

There's one spot I kept coming back to — not because it's special, but because no one really notices it. Quiet, isolated… easy to leave things behind.

I took a photo the last time I was there.

At first glance, it looks like nothing important.

But not everything is meant to be seen immediately.

Some things only reveal themselves if you look a little deeper.

— Sandhya
